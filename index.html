Features

Accurate currency estimates from multiple currencies (including cryptocurrencies) at daily price updates.

Relevant news concerning currencies to gain insights on potential events that could change existing currency rates.

Financial statistics and insights of currency rates based on price history that gives an idea of the currencies' potential state.

About Us

What inspired this project?

This project was inspired by an experience faced when trying to order components online for an hardware project. There was a stable budget estimate in US Dollars, however due to the flunctuating rates of my local currency at the period, I ended up spending more than I could have.


Minita is a project attempted to see if there is a way for for people to determine potential changes in currency rates and act accordingly
